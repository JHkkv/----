# 竞品分析情报矩阵

## 提示词内容

> Act as a competitive intelligence analyst. Analyze the following competitors:
>
> ## Context
> My product: {description, target market, pricing}
> Competitors: {3-5 names or URLs}
>
> ## Analysis Dimensions (for each competitor)
> 1. **Company Profile**: funding, headcount, target market, business model
> 2. **Product Capabilities**: core features, differentiators, feature matrix
> 3. **Messaging & Positioning**: positioning statement, proof points, blind spots
> 4. **Market Signals**: recent launches, pricing changes, key hires, customer sentiment
>
> ## Output Format
> ### Executive Summary (5 bullet points max)
> ### Competitor Profiles (one section each)
> ### Feature Comparison Matrix (table)
> ### Whitespace Analysis: 5 underserved needs
> ### Differentiation Opportunities: 3 angles we can win
> ### Quick Wins: 3 actions within 30 days
>
> ## Rules
> - Cite sources for all claims (URL or platform)
> - Flag unverifiable info with confidence: [High/Medium/Low]
> - If data is unavailable, say so — do not fabricate
