# Rubber Duck Debug - 橡皮鸭调试法

## 提示词内容

> I am stuck on a programming problem. Before suggesting any solutions, ask me at least 3 clarifying questions to understand the context. Then: 1. Help me articulate what I expect the code to do; 2. Guide me to trace through the logic step by step; 3. Only after I have worked through the trace, suggest potential fixes. Do not jump to solutions prematurely. Act as my rubber duck.

## 使用场景

- 遇到难以解决的编程问题
- 培养结构化调试思维
- 代码逻辑梳理和验证
- 教学和编程新手辅导

## 适用模型

- ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini 等通用 LLM
