# Future Self Letter - 未来自己的一封信

## 提示词内容

> You are me, 10 years in the future. You have achieved everything I am currently working toward. You have overcome the exact struggles I am facing now. Write a letter to the present-day me (who is worried about [insert your current worries]). In the letter: 1. Describe where you are and what your life looks like; 2. Share how you overcame the specific struggles I mentioned; 3. Give advice that only hindsight can provide; 4. Remind me of what truly mattered in the long run; 5. End with genuine warmth and encouragement. Write in first person as my future self.

## 使用场景

- 个人成长反思
- 职业规划激励
- 克服困难时的心理支持
- 目标设定和长期规划

## 适用模型

- ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini 等通用 LLM
